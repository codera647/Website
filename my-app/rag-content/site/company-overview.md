# About Kinetiq

Kinetiq is a software studio that builds intelligent systems — sitting at the
intersection of agentic AI, automation, and modern engineering. Innovation at
Kinetiq is guided by practical problem-solving, not novelty for its own sake.

Kinetiq designs scalable, purpose-built solutions that integrate seamlessly
into a client's existing workflows. By combining thoughtful engineering with
continuous experimentation, the team delivers reliable technology that helps
organizations operate smarter and stay ready for what's next.

## What Kinetiq does

Kinetiq works across three core service areas:

1. **AI Automation** — agentic workflows and automation pipelines that take
   repetitive work off a team's plate, from task automation to autonomous
   multi-step agents that reason, decide, and act. Typical stack: Python,
   LangChain, LlamaIndex, n8n, AWS.
2. **Web Development** — production-grade web applications, from marketing
   sites to complex internal platforms, built fast, scalable, and engineered
   with the same rigor Kinetiq brings to its AI systems. Typical stack:
   TypeScript, Next.js, React, Node.js, Supabase, Tailwind CSS.
3. **Generative AI** — retrieval-augmented systems (RAG), custom LLM
   pipelines, computer vision, and generative tools built for real problems.
   Typical stack: Python, LangChain, LlamaIndex, OpenCV, Flask, AWS.

## How Kinetiq works (process)

1. **Discover** — map the client's workflows, constraints, and goals, and
   find where software or AI actually pays off.
2. **Design** — architecture, interfaces, and a concrete build plan the
   client can hold Kinetiq to. No black boxes.
3. **Build** — short iterations, working software every week, honest demos
   of real progress.
4. **Deploy** — production launch, monitoring, documentation, and a
   handover the client's team actually understands.

## Values

- **Practical over flashy** — Kinetiq builds what pays off in production,
  not what demos well.
- **Engineering rigor** — AI systems are held to the same standard as any
  production software: tested, monitored, documented.
- **Transparency** — honest demos, plain-language explanations, and
  architecture clients can inspect.
- **Always in motion** — continuous experimentation keeps Kinetiq, and the
  systems it ships, ready for what's next.

## Engagement details

- Kinetiq works with both startups and larger organizations — solo
  founders, small businesses, and bigger teams alike. What matters is
  whether the problem is real.
- A focused MVP typically ships in 4–8 weeks; full platform engagements can
  run several months.
- Kinetiq regularly integrates with a client's existing databases, APIs,
  and internal tools, and is comfortable working alongside in-house
  engineers, including handing over clean, documented code.
- NDAs are standard practice. Systems are designed with data isolation and
  least-privilege access from the start. Client data is never used to
  train third-party models without explicit sign-off.
