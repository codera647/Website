# Kinetiq's work — project overview

Kinetiq has shipped production systems across AI automation, web
development, and generative AI. Summary of past projects:

## Synapse (2025) — Generative AI, RAG
A RAG-based knowledge and analytics platform that lets organizations
search, analyze, and interact with thousands of documents using AI. It
connects data sources like Google Drive, processes documents into secure
per-tenant libraries, and combines keyword, semantic, and contextual
retrieval with a reasoning layer that produces answers, summaries, charts,
and reports. Tags: RAG, Analytics, Multi-tenant. Notable figures:
thousands of documents indexed per library, three retrieval modes
combined, 100% tenant data isolation.

## Learn Quran Global (2025) — Web Development, EdTech
An online academy platform connecting students with qualified Quran
teachers worldwide, with scheduling, progress tracking, and payments
included. Delivered in a two-month engagement; a stable production
platform students and teachers use daily. Client quote: "Working with
KINETIQ was a smooth and reassuring experience. They clearly understood
our vision and translated it into a clean, easy-to-use website. The
platform runs reliably, and the team was responsive throughout." —
Hammad Sarwar, Learn Quran Global.

## Records (2025) — Web Development, Desktop/Fintech
A custom desktop application for jewelry businesses to manage khata
(credit/debit) records, with automatic balance calculation and reporting.
Built with input from actual jewelry business owners: customer profiles,
transaction tracking, automatic balance updates, reporting, and
backup/recovery. Delivered in two months; used daily in production.
Client quote: "KINETIQ developed a custom desktop application for our
jewelry business. The system simplified our daily operations and reduced
manual errors. The software is practical, stable, and has made
record-keeping much easier." — Qaisar Abbas, Records.
