# Frequently asked questions

**What does a typical engagement look like?**
Most projects follow Kinetiq's four-step process: a short discovery phase to
map workflows and goals, a design phase with a concrete build plan,
iterative development with working software every week, and a production
deployment with documentation and handover. Typical engagements run from 4
weeks for a focused build to several months for a full platform.

**Do you work with startups or only enterprises?**
Both. Kinetiq has shipped for solo founders, small businesses, and larger
organizations. What matters is that the problem is real — if software or AI
genuinely pays off for the workflow, Kinetiq is interested.

**How fast can you ship?**
A focused MVP typically ships in 4–8 weeks. Kinetiq works in short
iterations with honest demos of real progress, so clients see working
software from the first weeks, not a big reveal at the end.

**Can you work with our existing systems and team?**
Yes. Kinetiq regularly integrates with existing databases, APIs, and
internal tools, and is comfortable working alongside in-house engineers,
including handing over clean, documented code the client's team can own.

**How do you handle confidentiality and data security?**
NDAs are standard practice. Kinetiq designs with data isolation and
least-privilege access from the start. For AI systems, client data is never
used to train third-party models without explicit sign-off.
